Dress code

Cocktail.

We'd love our guests to look dapper and sexy but we don't expect jackets or ties - it will be midsummer after all!

Getting there

We suggest using Uber to travel in small groups together. The distances are not far (by design!) so it should be affordable (and safer) to share rides. There will also be on-street parking with security at both venues.

If you are new to Uber you may use the code ny425 to get a discount on your first ride.

If you would prefer a more traditional cab service we recommend Excite Taxis or Rikkis.

Accomodation

For family and friends travelling from up country or abroad, there is ample accommodation available in Cape Town but we suggest you book this soon - prices and availabilty during high season can be tricky. If you would like us to put you in touch with other guests to arrange shared accommodation please let us know.

We can recommend airbnb and Booking.com if you'd like to book accommdation online.

Kids

While the ceremony will be children-friendly we would not recommend bringing little people to the party afterwards. If you need help organising babysitting please let us know. We will make babysitting arrangements for all our guests travelling with small children from out of town.

Food

We will ensure that we provide vegetarian and vegan food options. If you have other special requirements please let us know.
